CaYPT 2020

 

To participate to the 2020 Canadian Young Physicists’ Tournament, Leo made a mechanism that observed the motion and the friction of the oscillator.

The topic he researched is: A massive object is placed onto two identical parallel horizontal cylinders. The two cylinders each rotate with the same angular velocity, but in opposite directions. Investigate how the motion of the object on the cylinders depends on the relevant parameters.

SHAD 2020

 

Leo was part of the SHAD 2020 Canopus Campus, and competed in team 4BIT to create an investigation exploring the effects of microgravity on the diffusion of particles. Out of 6 teams, his team was nominated to represent the Canopus Campus, and was presented to judges with the remaining 9 campuses. Leo is a current SHAD Alumni.
