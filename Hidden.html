HIDDEN

HIDDEN

 

In 2020, Leo generated a simple practical and effective technology that can eliminate ink marks like bar codes, QR codes, and sensitive information that is labelled on all types of thermal paper.

This is a pen shape device that has three main features. First, it features a smart circuit that contains three resistors that generate 1.6 watts of heat each, a thermal fuse to detect temperature at all times and prevent over heating, and an intuitive user-friendly switch button. The second feature is the pen tip. When connected to the smart circuit that provides resistance, it is able to melt ink on all types of thermal paper when the temperature is reaching to a specific degree. The third feature is our user-friendly and compact design that houses all of the components. This device targets three main problem: First, it aims to protect information privacy by eliminating inked codes like QR or bar codes on all types of label. It is instrumentally different from permanent marker that only covers the code which can still be tracked later on.

HIDDEN will completely destroy the codes by melting the coating material inside the labeling paper. Secondly, HIDDEN is targeted at improving the efficiency of removing QR/Bar codes in the labelling paper. Among the hundreds of different types of packaging, there are certain labels that simply cannot be torn away. To eliminate the codes on these types of label is to demolish the code. Thirdly, HIDDEN is the ultimate solution to provide security which is affordable at selling price of $8.99 each so that all consumers can benefit from it. The solution let Leo qualify DECA reginal competition and move up to 2021 DECA Ontario Innovation Business Plan competition that will be held on middle of February 2021.
