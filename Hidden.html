HIDDEN

HIDDEN

 

During the COVID-19 pandemic, more and more consumers pursue online shopping, receiving many delivery packages almost daily. The inked QR/BAR codes on the packaging label are full of business and personal information. In order to protect the information and enhance privacy, consumers would usually tear off the label or cover the label with markers before they throw the packaging into the recycling bins. But in some cases, those labels cannot be torn off and marked away from the packages. To solve this issue and bring in convenience, Leo created a solution that can effectively eliminate the inked BAR/QR codes.

This is a pen-shaped device named Hidden. It can generate heat to melt the inks on the label. It features a smart circuit that contains resistors to generate heat power. The device features a thermal fuse to constantly monitor and detect temperatures to prevent overheating, along with an intuitive switch button. In addition, the pen tip is designed to be able to melt inks on all types of thermal paper when the temperature reaches a specific degree. With this device and just a small scratch, consumers can easily eliminate the information on the labels, protecting their private information, at anytime and anywhere.

HIDDEN will compete in the 2021 DECA Ontario Championships.
