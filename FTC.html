St. Robert CHS Team 14688

In 2019-2020 FTC season, Leo was the school club president and team captain for FTC team 16488, where he led, designed, and built the competitive robot while training new team members. With the help of team mentors and members, team 16488 won the Inspire Award at the 2020 Ontario FTC provincial championship, qualifying the team to the 2020 FRC World Championships in Detroit.

Awards:

  • Team 14688 won the Inspire Award in 2020 Ontario FTC competition and qualified the team to 2020 FTC World Championship competition.
