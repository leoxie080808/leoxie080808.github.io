St. Robert CHS Team 14688

St. Robert CHS Team 14688

In 2019-2020 FTC season, Leo was the school club president and team captain for FTC team 16488, where he led, designed, and built the competitive robot while training new team members. With the help of team mentors and members, team 16488 won the Inspire Award at the 2020 Ontario FTC provincial championship, qualifying the team to the 2020 FRC World Championships in Detroit.

Awards:

  • Team 14688 won the Inspire Award in 2020 Ontario FTC competition and qualified the team to 2020 FTC World Championship competition.

    • 2019-2020 FIRST Tech Challenge

    Motor encoders for PID feedback loop during autonomous for precise movements

    String system driven with custom pulleys and elevator bearings

    Designed double green jointed custom intakes, using custom polycarbonate shells and flex wheels for additional grip, driven using VEX system parts

    Line trackers mounted on side arm for stone detection and manipulation during autonomous

    Designed low profile cascading elevator for 4 stone height capacity

    Presenting and explaining the design process to judges at the FTC provincial championships

    Competing in kickoff qualification round with successful autonomous

    Winning the Provincial Inspire Award at the Ontario Provincial Championships
